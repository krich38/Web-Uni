UNE Sports


The University of New England has an indoor sports centre, which is considered one of the best university sporting precincts in Australia.
The Sport Centre has the following features:

  • Gym

  • 15 hectares of playing fields

  • Indoor multi-purpose halls: squash, badminton, table tennis, basketball and netball courts

  • Heated 25m indoor pool

  • Bellevue Grandstand

  • Creche

  • Cafe

  • Group Fitness Rooms

  • Indoor climbing wall (under renovation so closed until further notice)

  • Outdoor netball, basketball, and tennis courts


The pool is a heated, 6 lane, 25 metre indoor pool is open to the public and hosts a variety of activities throughout the week including lap swimming, Aqua Aerobics, Harwood Swim School (contact: harwoodswimming@bigpond.com) and water polo.

UNE has an appointed official sports doctor, Dr, Madgwick (02) 6773 3856
